\section{NPCs}

\subsection{Deer "0"}
An ordinary deer, wanders in the forest, does not harm the player.

\subsection{NOT Deer "1"}
A glitchy deer, an SCP(6448), appears and vanishes suddenly, confuses the player, harmless but if seen with a normal Deer, it becomes a XOR Deer!

\subsection{Mysterious Figure}
A mysterious figure, watches the player.
